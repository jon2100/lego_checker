Features
An underwater utopia
Cultivate a thriving aquarium full of boldly beautiful tropical fish, vibrant water plants and even a few surprises. Breathe liveliness into the set with hidden levers to move fish and plants like real sea creatures. A unique display model of a brick-built underwater paradise for your home or office without the upkeep.
Be your own curator of a thriving aquarium
Measuring over 20.5 in. (52 cm) wide, this highly detailed brick-built tropical aquarium can transform a small corner in your living space into a unique, meditative display.
See the surprises inside this inventive set
Move the Sea Crab

Turn the ‘stone’ element to make the crab come out.

Swirl the Corals

Rotate a grey knob to make these sea anemones move.

Open the Treasure Chest

Turn the crank behind it to make the lid open and close.

Animate the striped brickfish

The side crank makes the fish wiggle and ‘swim.’

Charming and carefree LEGO® creatures
Add a splash of imagination with a rainbowfish, angelfish varieties and a special striped brickfish created by the LEGO® Designers.
