    Space: The Final Frontier.

    "These are the voyages of the Starship Enterprise. Its continuing mission: to explore strange new worlds; to seek out new life and new civilisations; to boldly go where no one has gone before."

    Ready for adventure?

    Join Captain Jean-Luc Picard and his crew aboard the legendary U.S.S. Enterprise NCC-1701-D™ in the first ever LEGO® Star Trek™ set. A detailed replica of the legendary starship with a detachable command saucer, opening shuttle bay, angled display stand and much more.

    Meet the crew

    8. Guinan

      "My name is Guinan. I tend bar and I listen."

    9. Wesley Crusher

      "We've only chartered 19% of the galaxy."

    An out-of-this-world display piece for Star Trek fans

    This authentically detailed build comes with an angled display stand that makes it perfect for showcasing your fandom, along with a cast of minifigures for endless storytelling possibilities.
