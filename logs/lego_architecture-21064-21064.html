    Paris – City of Love
    $79.99

    Embrace your fascination with Paris and its iconic landmarks with this collectible LEGO® Architecture Paris – City of Love (21064) home decor building set for women, men and adults ages 18 and up. Create a display piece that makes a stunning architectural gift idea for you or any history or travel lover. Explore the starry sky and cityscape as you build. Discover the details of the Eiffel Tower, Notre-Dame de Paris Cathedral, the Arc de Triomphe and the Louvre. Finish the set with the decorated ‘City of Love’ brick and the brick-built frame, then hang it on a wall or display it on a shelf in your home or office.

    As you create the framed wall decor artwork, enjoy enhanced building with the LEGO Builder app, zooming and rotating with 3D instructions while saving and tracking progress – all from within the app. This premium set will appeal to you or any creative adult who loves history, travel or Paris architecture, and it makes a great gift for LEGO building fans. Set contains 958 pieces.

    • ICONIC LANDMARKS BUILDING SET – Adults ages 18 and up can embrace their love of architecture with this LEGO® building set, which makes great home decor to display in a living room, home or office
    • EXPLORE PARIS IN DETAIL – This detailed LEGO® home decor set of Paris – City of Love (21064) features 4 Paris architecture icons amid a city background and sky full of stars, framed in white bricks
    • STANDING OR HANGING FRAMED DISPLAY – Recreate the Eiffel Tower, Notre-Dame de Paris, the Arc de Triomphe and the Louvre, with a starlit city background in a frame, to hang up or stand on a shelf
    • BUILD AND DISPLAY – This relaxing activity for adults lets you build the framed artwork, attach the decorated ‘City of Love’ brick to the front and display it as wall or home decor anywhere
    • GIFT IDEA FOR ADULTS – The detailed architecture set is ideal for travel and history lovers, and fans of Paris or LEGO® collectible items, plus it makes a great gift choice for birthdays or holidays
    • COLLECT YOUR BUILDS – With the LEGO® Builder app, you can build your models using 3D instructions, track your progress and save all your sets within the app
    • LEGO® ARCHITECTURE SETS – This home decor item is part of a collection of premium-quality building sets (each sold separately) for women, men and adults like you who enjoy hands-on creative activities
    • DIMENSIONS – With 958 pieces to inspire creativity and calm, this artistic representation of the Paris skyline measures over 11.5 in. (30 cm) high, 10.5 in. (27 cm) wide and 2 in. (5 cm) deep
