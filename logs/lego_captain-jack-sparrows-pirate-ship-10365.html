    Meet the ship’s crew

    1. Hector Barbossa & Jack Sparrow

    2. Will Turner & Elizabeth Swann

    3. Gibbs & Anamaria

    4. Cotton & Marty

    Check out the details around the ship

    1. Relax in the Captain’s Quarters

      Open the deck to reveal the Captain’s Quarters with furnishings, candlesticks and an apple.

    2. Hidden Treasures

      Keep the pirate loot away from prying eyes in secret compartments.

    3. The figurehead leads the way

      Decorate the bow with the winged figure to guide your journey.

    Capture the ship in all its glory

    Fans of Disney’s Pirates of the Caribbean can build and display a detailed replica of the legendary ghost galleon.

    Fire away!

    Features rows of port and starboard cannons that deploy at the turn of a dial.
